WHAT ARE WE ?

We provide hospitality to dogs when they need it.
Enjoy our day-monthly passes from woofly packages offered at different prices.
We have all the special treatment ranging from therapeutic sessions, training sessions, grooming sessions and we are also kind enough to take in special favors to make your dogs feel more comfortable with us.

VISION & MISSION

Our aim is to grow into an organization that promotes dog rights,
hosting dog shelter for dogs that are being poorly treated in the country.

We aim to partner with dog insurance companies for the expansion of our business. We also plot on having annual dog fest where families and children sign their dogs up for competitions based on their responsiveness, agility, speed, and talent.

HISTORY

We were inspired by the longing dog owners had for such a service back in 2014; Our HQ is located at a shop 7 Midrand 1687 waterfall city Our research indicated tha South Africa is lagging in the pet care industry and such a business will grow into one that provides and promoted proper pet care. We cover for those who are hesitant on leaving their dogs at home alone, we are confident that this type of service will be convenient to you as a pet owner.
